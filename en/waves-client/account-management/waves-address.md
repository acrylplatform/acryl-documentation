# Where Can I find my Waves address?

Open your Waves wallet. At the top righ corner click on the **Account avatar**.

![](/_assets/waves_address_01.png)

Here you will find the Waves address of your account. To copy address to clipboard, just click on the **—Åopy** button ![](/_assets/waves_address_02.png).
